Moodlamp

Remember those glowing plastic bugs taht sat in the corner of the room slowly changing from one colour to the next? Here's an emulation of that for WebOS smartphones. It has a variety of modes to suit your every mood!

Current modes are:

  • Multicoloured (change from red -> yellow -> green -> cyan -> blue -> magenta -> red)
  • Shine from black to red and back
  • Shine from black to green and back
  • Shine from black to blue and back
